Lemon-Garlic Shrimp

Ingredients

  1. 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
  2. 16 large shrimp - peeled, deveined, and tails on, or more to taste
  3. 3 large cloves garlic, smashed, or more to taste
  4. ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
  5. 2 teaspoons seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®), or to taste
  6. salt and ground black pepper to taste
  7. 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  8. 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  9. 3 teaspoons lemon zest
