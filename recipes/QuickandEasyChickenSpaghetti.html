Quick and Easy Chicken Spaghetti

Ingredients

  1. 1 (12 ounce) package angel hair pasta
  2. 2 cups chopped cooked chicken breast
  3. 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  4. 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
  5. ¾ (8 ounce) package processed cheese, cubed
  6. ½ (4 ounce) jar sliced mushrooms, drained
  7. salt and pepper to taste
